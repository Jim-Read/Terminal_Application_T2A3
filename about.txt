A simple application build around Open Notify API that gets the co ordinates of the ISS

A user can track the location of the ISS and gets infromation as to its location

You can also get the pass times in your local time and date of when the ISS will next
pass by - times are approx and give a rise time - as in when it appears above the horizon
this thing really wizzes by

Also you can measure the distance from your location and the iss - this does not measure the 
altitude but more as if it was on the ground and takes the curve of the earth into account

Finally you can see which astronauts are on board

There is a GUI Front of of the terminal application
